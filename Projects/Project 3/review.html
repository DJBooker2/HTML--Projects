My Review

The dark knight is my favorite movie based on the simple but powerful message that it provides. It is about a man who literally lost everything and was forced to wake up and face what the world has to offer. Instead of choosing to let it cosume him and give up, he became a beacon of hope that everyone can look up to. Yes, his methods wern't, how do you say "traditional", but at the end of the day he never killed anyone. Even after all the terrible things that the joker did he still couldnt kill him because it wasn't the right thing to do. Even in darkness you can not loose yourself. That is something i had to learn in the military and its something ill never forget.
